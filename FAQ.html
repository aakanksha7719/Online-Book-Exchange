BOOK EXCHANGE FAQS


How long do I have to wait to receive the books I Request?
Once you have submitted your request, we will process it within 24 hours, and then ship it to you.


I received the wrong book. What can I do?
If you receive the book you did not request, please contact us so we can rectify the situation as soon as possible.


I have requested a book, but the person who offered the book has not fulfilled the request and is not replying to my messages?
If you feel that you have been waiting for your order too long and the person sending it to you is not replying to your messages then you can always cancel your request.


Can I order or send books to you from outside the country?
International swapping and delivery are not yet available through our website. We hope to add this option in the future.


How do I notify you of changes to my mailing address and email address?
If you wish to change any information on your profile, Sign up or register to our website again, and you can update your information.


How soon can I expect a response after contacting youy website?
You can expect to hear from us within 24 to 48 hours.
